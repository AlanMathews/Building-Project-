What Are The Types Of Roofing Material

Slates

Roofing slates are stone slabs made out of slate, which are used as roofing tiles. They are the primary product of the slate industry.

Clay Tiles

Roof tiles are designed mainly to keep out rain, and are traditionally made from locally available materials such as terracotta .

Thatching

Thatching is the craft of building a roof with dry vegetation such as straw, water reed, rushes, heather, or palm branches, layering the vegetation so as to shed water away from the inner roof
